About Elekko Agrovet & Farms Ltd

Elekko Agrovet & Farms Ltd is a leading provider of quality veterinary and agricultural products in Nigeria. Our mission is to support farmers, livestock owners, and pet lovers with the best products, tools, and advice for healthy animals and productive farms. We specialize in veterinary pharmaceuticals, animal feeds, day-old chicks, farm equipment, and more.

We believe in combining innovation, quality, and affordability to empower agriculture across the nation. Our team of experts is always ready to guide you with tips, recommendations, and customer support to ensure your farming or pet care journey is a success.
